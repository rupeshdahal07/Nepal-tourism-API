Trekking Safety Tips

  • Always check the latest trail conditions before starting your trek
  • Carry enough water and stay hydrated
  • Inform someone of your trekking itinerary
  • Hire a licensed guide for unfamiliar trails
  • Respect local customs and protected areas
  • Carry a first aid kit and emergency supplies
  • Be prepared for sudden weather changes
